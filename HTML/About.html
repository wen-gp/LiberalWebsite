Who is Liberal?
We are a technological innovation enterprise born out of Zhejiang University. Known as a national high-tech enterprise, we are the fourth batch of "Small Giant" enterprises of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, and a Special Innovation Enterprise of Zhejiang Province.

Our company has more than 210 employees, including 4 overseas talents, 4 professors, and 2 associate professors. There are also 12 doctors, and more than 86% of employees with a bachelor degree or above.

We are committed to intelligent manufacturing, superior equipment, intelligent sensing, intelligent detection and other fields. Most of the company’s products are independently developed, and the domestic market share ranks in the forefront of the industry, breaking th long-term monopoly of foreign companies.

We are a national high-tech enterprise integrating scientific research, product development, engineering design, and technical consulting. Besides, the company has obtained 45 invention patents, 29 utility model patents, 10 software copyrights, and 4 registered trademarks.

We take "Created by China, Create China" as our ideal, and we are committed to building a century-old national brand. To become a well-known leading technology and strength enterprise in China’s high-end equipment and intelligent inspection industries is the development goal of our "liberal People".
